The Blessed Life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Early Life Prophethood Teachings Legacy

Early Life

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ was born in Makkah in 570 CE, known as the Year of the Elephant. He was from the noble tribe of Quraysh. From his early years, he was known as Al-Ameen (the trustworthy) due to his honesty and character.

Prophethood

At the age of 40, in the cave of Hira, Angel Jibreel (Gabriel) came with the first revelation of the Qur’an. This marked the beginning of his mission to call humanity towards the Oneness of Allah and righteous living.

Teachings

The Prophet ﷺ taught compassion, justice, forgiveness, humility, and worship of Allah alone. His sayings (Hadith) and actions (Sunnah) are guiding principles for Muslims worldwide.

Legacy

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ changed the course of human history. Through his message of Islam, billions of people have embraced the path of peace, truth, and justice. His life is a role model for all humanity.
